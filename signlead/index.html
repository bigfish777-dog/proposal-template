My takeaways

Pete,

Good chat on the phone. Here's what stuck with me:

You've built something genuinely impressive. Seven years in, five employees, a workshop expansion on the way, 2 million views on TikTok for a signage company in County Durham. That doesn't happen by accident.

But the bit that really got my attention was when you talked about the agency work. The design agencies who bring you in on B2B projects, the new builds, the rebrand rollouts, the full signage packages worth £3k, £5k, £10k, £20k a pop. That's where the real money is - but you know that already.

The challenge isn't whether you can deliver that work. You clearly can.

The challenge is that right now, those relationships happen when they happen.

There's no system behind it. No proactive outreach. No consistent presence in front of the people who send you the best jobs.

You're not short of talent or ambition. You're short of a pipeline.

And that's exactly what we'd build together.

The opportunity

Let's look at the numbers.

Right now, your average job might be £1,000 to £3,000. That's fine for the bread-and-butter work. But the agency channel is a completely different animal.

One design agency relationship that sends you two or three B2B signage projects a year could be worth £10,000 to £60,000 in annual revenue. Not from cold leads who haggle on price, but from warm introductions where you're the recommended supplier.

Get five of those relationships working properly and you've added a quarter of a million to the top line. That changes the shape of the business entirely.

The question isn't whether those agencies exist. They do. There's hundreds of design agencies, branding studios, interior designers, and architects across the North East alone who regularly need a signage partner they can trust.

The question is: how do you get in front of them, stay in front of them, and become the name they think of when the next project lands on their desk?

That's what this proposal is about.

What we'd do together

Two things. Both simple. Both powerful.

1

Weekly consulting

You'll get a weekly call with me or Ad. Not a vague "catchup" where we ask you how things are going and nod thoughtfully. A proper working session.

We'll use these to:

  • Build your target list of design agencies, branding studios, interior designers, and architects
  • Define your messaging and positioning for the trade/agency market
  • Plan your outreach strategy so you're not just emailing into a void
  • Review what's working, what's not, and adjust
  • Work through your TikTok and LinkedIn content strategy
  • Tackle whatever else needs tackling that week, whether that's a quote follow-up, a pitch deck, or a tricky client conversation

You'll also get our personal phone numbers. WhatsApp us whenever you need to bounce something, sense-check an idea, or get a second opinion. We're not precious about it.

This isn't coaching. We're not going to ask you what you think the answer is. We'll tell you what we'd do, based on 25+ years of doing this across hundreds of businesses. You decide whether to run with it.

2

Weekly emails (written by us)

This is the engine.

Once we've built your target list of agencies, we'll help you get them onto an email list. By doing it properly, one relationship at a time, so every person on that list actually wants to hear from you.

Then, every week, we'll write you an email. Then we'll send it out of your email marketing tool.

Not a sales email. Not a newsletter full of clip art and "10% off this month!" Not something that sounds like AI wrote it at 2am.

A proper, human, anecdotal email. The kind that a creative director at a design agency actually reads and thinks "I like these guys."

The kind of email that, three months in, means when a project lands that needs signage, your name is the first one that comes to mind.

We've done this for clients across dozens of industries. It works. Consistently. Because most businesses either don't email at all, or they email badly. We do it well, and we do it every single week without fail.

What we're not doing

I want to be upfront about what this isn't, because I think you've had enough people promise you the world.

We're not running your ads. You don't need paid traffic right now. You need relationships.

We're not managing your social media. You're already doing a decent job of that yourself, especially on TikTok. We'll advise on it during our weekly calls, but we're not taking it over.

We're not redesigning your website. It could do with some love, but that's not the priority. The priority is getting in front of the right people and building those agency relationships.

We're not building you a funnel. Your business doesn't sell through funnels. It sells through trust, reputation, and being the obvious choice when a project needs signage.

What we are doing is giving you a senior marketing brain on a weekly call, and writing the emails that will build you a pipeline of agency relationships over time.

Simple. Focused. Effective.
