How We Actually Work Right Now

Let me walk you
through a typical job.

1

New client meeting ~4 hrs

Recorded on Fathom. In person. Deliverables agreed. Everyone leaves with a different version of the plan in their head.

2

Grace builds the project plan ~2-3 hrs

Uploads recording to Claude or ChatGPT. Manually builds a Google Sheet with deliverables, owners, due dates. Done from scratch every time.

3

Ad or Gabriella picks up the work variable

Each opens their own AI. Different tools, different accounts, different prompts, different context. Different output — every time.

4

Output collated and sent to client ~30-60 min

Copied into Google Docs, formatted differently by whoever's on. Grace updates the project sheet. Hopefully. Client gets an email. Maybe.

5

Client feedback and chasing ~46 min avg per call

Via email. Or WhatsApp. Or inside the doc. Or to Grace. Or directly to Ad. We don't know where it'll land. And then they book a call to ask what's next. Every single time.

The real problem

Different members of the team have different approaches, different levels of effectiveness in prompting, different amounts of context they provide. And the quality of the output varies dramatically — not because of talent, but because nobody has the same picture.

And this only gets worse at scale

Candace's agency — bigger team, more people, all using ChatGPT differently. Some on Pro, some on free. Every single one doing it their own way. The variation in output quality is enormous. We're heading in the same direction if we don't fix this now.
