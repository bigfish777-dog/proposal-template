The situation

Joel,

I'll be straight with you.

When I looked at your online presence before our first conversation, I made a snap judgement. I assumed you were brand new. Early stage. Not ready for what we do.

And you've told me that's exactly the problem. Other people are reaching the same conclusion. Members' clubs, corporate wellness teams, HR departments at banks. They're looking you up, and what they're seeing doesn't match what you actually deliver.

Your team is outstanding. You said that yourself, and I believe you. But right now, the marketing is letting the team down.

The good news? This isn't a "we need to rebuild everything from scratch" situation. It's a cosmetics job. A sprint to close the gap between how good you actually are and how good you look to someone who's never met you.

That's what this proposal covers.

The plan.

This is a 30-day fixed project. Not a retainer. Not a commitment to ongoing work. A sprint with a clear brief: make Vyrsion look like the established, credible operation it actually is.

PHASE 1
Phase 1: Positioning Session

Week 1

Before we touch anything visual, we need to understand who you're trying to impress and what "credible" looks like in their world.

90-minute strategy session (virtual)

We'll dig into: Who your ideal corporate clients actually are. Not just "companies" but which types, which roles, which decision makers. What your team's credentials and track record look like when presented properly. You mentioned banking experience. That's gold if we frame it right. What a corporate buyer or members' club expects to see when they Google you or check your socials. The messaging that makes someone think "these people are serious" rather than "this looks like it started last week".

PHASE 2
Phase 2: Fix the First Impression

Weeks 2-3

We're not rebuilding your website. We're rewriting the key pages so they read like an established practice, not a startup.

Website Messaging Overhaul

Homepage, about page, services page. Corporate-facing language. Team credentials pulled forward. Proof points made visible. When a Head of Wellbeing at a bank lands on your site, they should immediately think "these people know what they're doing".

LinkedIn Profile Optimisation

Your personal LinkedIn needs to position you as a corporate wellness authority. Not a founder who just launched something. We'll rewrite your headline, about section, and featured content so that when someone looks you up after a meeting, they see exactly what they need to see.

Instagram & Social Tidy-Up

A handful of high-quality posts you can pin and feature. Not a content calendar. Not an ongoing commitment. Just enough credibility content that a casual scroll through your profiles reinforces the right impression. Think case study snippets, team credentials, client outcomes.

PHASE 3
Phase 3: Leave-Behind Materials

Weeks 3-4

A professionally designed PDF (print-ready too, if you want hard copies) that explains who you are, what your team delivers, and why it matters.

Capability Document

Something you can email to an HR director after a conversation, or leave behind at a members' club meeting. This isn't a marketing brochure full of fluff. It's a serious document for serious people making decisions about their employees' wellbeing. It should make the recipient think "right, this is a proper outfit".

  • LinkedIn profile rewrite - Positioning you as the authority in your space
  • Social content pieces - 3-5 pieces designed to establish credibility on Instagram and LinkedIn
  • Capability document - PDF, print-ready, you can email or hand out at meetings
  • WhatsApp access - To our team throughout the project. Questions answered same day.

Your investment.

This is a fixed-fee project, not an ongoing retainer.

To put that in context: you've been reaching out on Bark and speaking to other agencies. How much time have you spent on that so far? And how many of them actually understood what you needed?
